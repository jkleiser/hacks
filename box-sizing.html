box-sizing: initial

This is a demonstration of some variants and combinations of the box-sizing property. The interesting point here is that there are browsers that seem to ignore the box-sizing: initial variant. In the pink case below, this causes the div to be of the same width as the border-box case above it. Internet Explorer 11 is one browser where this happens.
